Context:

My goal with this project is to recreate the mechanics and feel of Viscera Cleanup Details.

To achieve this, I spend a lot of time studying how they worked and how to replicate them to capture the game's unique atmosphere.

Blood System:

First, I had to make what make Viscera so... Viscera... the Blood.

I used blood textures to create decals and made them fade with a random noise effect by adjusting an opacity parameter that decrease with each mop hit.

This ensure a full workability while also adding some variation to the blood fading, preventing any redundancy.

Mop:

I quickly recreated the mop in Maya to have a solid base to work with.

I then attached it to a Player Socket to ensure good positioning and, when player hits, applied physics to the area and remove blood.

I followed the same setup as the original game by creating eight dirt stages to display blood and droplets, in order to recreate the same feel.

(Texture are in WIP)

Inventory System:

Since the game automatically adds item to the Player's Inventory at spawn, I decided to make it dynamic to fully customize what items you're carrying.

Players have now an unlimited amount of slot that can be filled with the Mop, Blood Captor, Broom and your bare hands to hold objects.

I also spend some time adjusting assets' physics to match the one in the original game.

(More objects will be added such as Broom and Blood Captor)
